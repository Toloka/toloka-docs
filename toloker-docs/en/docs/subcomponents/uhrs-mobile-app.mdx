# How to download the UHRS mobile App

## Android
1. Join the Google group: Make sure you’re logged in to your Google account, then follow [this link](https://groups.google.com/g/uhrs-tolokers/) or scan the QR code below to join the UHRS Tolokers group.
<Image width="350px" withBackground imageId="f9089d9ae1f384433ca62e6b378a1015" alt="" />

2. Download the app: After about 15 minutes of joining the group, you should be able to access the UHRS app on the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.uhrs%20). Do not log in yet!


## iOS
Download the app: Simply head to the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ru/app/uhrs/id1636040053?l=en-GB) to get the UHRS app. Do not log in yet!

You can also use the QR code below.
<Image width="350px" withBackground imageId="aeccf17dd8aee728c09c4c341123e8cc" alt="" />

## Logging in using the QR code
1. Head to Toloka, find a UHRS task, and go to the UHRS platform.

1. On your first UHRS visit via Toloka, you’ll be presented with a QR code. Scan the QR code to get authorized in the UHRS mobile app.
