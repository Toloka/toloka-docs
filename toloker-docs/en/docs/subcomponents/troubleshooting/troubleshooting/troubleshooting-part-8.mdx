<Cut title="How do I hide a project?">

Go to the main page, find the project you need, click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the project card, and select **Hide this project**. This project will no longer be displayed on the main page.

To return it to the main page again, follow these steps:

1. Go to the projects page.

1. In the filter on the right, put a check mark next to **Hidden**.

1. Find the project you need, click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the project card, and select **Show this project again**.

</Cut>

<Cut title="Projects are not loaded in the app">

Try updating the app, reinstalling it, and restarting your mobile device.

<Note mode="warning" title="Warning">

Reinstalling the app will clear the history of completed projects. You can view the full history of completed projects and earnings on the [Toloka](https://toloka.ai/tolokers/en/) website.

</Note>

</Cut>
