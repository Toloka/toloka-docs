To view your results, go to the **Done** page.

There you can view the full history of completed tasks (total earnings and the number of accepted and rejected tasks), as well as statistics for the day. To view statistics, click the button with the number of completed tasks next to the date.

<TolokersPriemkaContent2/>

After the review, payment for the accepted tasks is credited to your account. The <img src="https://tlk-infra-front.azureedge.net/portal-static/uploaded/images/1676733322566/1676733322566_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> amounts are indicated in the upper part of the menu (the <img src="https://tlk-infra-front.azureedge.net/portal-static/uploaded/images/1676733318631/1676733318631_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> icon):

- Gray indicates the amount for tasks that are currently under review.

- Green indicates money on your Toloka account.

To view your earnings history, go to the **My money → Earnings** page. It shows the list of accepted tasks with the date and amount.

<TolokersPriemkaContent3/>

1. Go to the **Done** page.

1. Open the rejected task.

1. Click <img src="https://tlk-infra-front.azureedge.net/portal-static/uploaded/images/1676448516853/1676448516853_original_jpeg.jpeg" alt="" /> in the task card and select **Contact requester**.

1. Describe briefly why you think the task was completed correctly. The task number is attached to the message automatically.

1. Click **Submit**.
