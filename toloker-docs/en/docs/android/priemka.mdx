To view your results, go to the **Done** page.

There you can view the full history of completed task sets (total earnings and the number of accepted and rejected task sets), as well as statistics for the day. To view statistics, click the button with the number of completed task sets next to the date.

<TolokersPriemkaContent2/>

After the review, payment for the accepted task sets is credited to your account. The <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1677847618206/1677847618206_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> amounts are indicated in the upper part of the menu (the <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1677847612879/1677847612879_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> icon):

- Gray indicates the amount for task sets that are currently under review.

- Green indicates money on your Toloka account.

To view your earnings history, go to the **My money → Earnings** page. It shows the list of accepted task sets with the date and amount.

<TolokersPriemkaContent3/>

1. Go to the **Done** page.

1. Open the rejected task set.

1. Click <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733319521/1676733319521_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> in the task set card and select **Contact requester**.

1. Describe briefly why you think the task set was completed correctly. The task set number is attached to the message automatically.

1. Click **Submit**.
