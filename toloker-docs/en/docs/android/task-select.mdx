# Selecting a task

- [Field task](#walk)

## Regular (non-field) tasks {#common}

To complete a non-field task:

1. Open the list of tasks by tapping **<img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → Available**.

1. Read the instructions and go to the task.

1. Tap **Start**.

1. Answer the questions and tap **Done**.

You will be redirected to a page with the next task. To exit the task, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733319521/1676733319521_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> at the top of the screen and choose **Finish**.

## How to save uncompleted tasks {#howtosave}

You can save tasks that you didn't complete in **Reserved**. To do this:

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Settings**.

1. Select the **Always reserve tasks to complete later** option

To continue performing the task, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Reserved**, find the task you need, and tap **Resume**.

## Field tasks {#fieldwork}

To complete a field task:

1. Open the task list. To do this, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Available**.

1. Use one of these ways to open task locations:

    * Select a task in the task list and tap **Open map** to see the locations for this task.

    * Open the common map by tapping <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448795481/1676448795481_original_png.png" alt="" /> at the bottom of the screen to view locations with available tasks.

    To return to the task list, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448711664/1676448711664_original_png.png" alt="" /> at the bottom of the screen.

1. Tap a placemark with a task.

    If there are several tasks in one spot, use the search function. To find a task in the list:

    1. Pull the list up.

    1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733320430/1676733320430_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> on the top right of the screen.

    1. Start entering the task name or description.

    To set a minimum price per task:

    1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448913193/1676448913193_original_png.png" alt="" /> at the bottom of the screen.

    1. Move the slider.

    1. Tap **Apply**.

1. Read the instructions.

1. Reserve a task by tapping **Reserve**. The task is displayed on the **Reserved** page. The countdown starts from this moment. The timer is displayed in the task header next to the <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733320837/1676733320837_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> icon. Max duration is specified in the task card.

    You can reserve several tasks at once.

1. To build a route, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448902899/1676448902899_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Get directions**. The route is created in the [Yandex Maps](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/yandex-maps/id313877526) app.

1. Go to the location shown in the task. Next, select the desired task on the **Reserved** and tap **Continue**.

1. Perform the task:

    * Re-read the instructions.

    * Answer the questions and attach a photo (if required).

    * Tap **Done**.

        <Note mode="warning" title="Warning">

        You need to tap **Done** while you are at the location shown in the task. Otherwise, your response might be rejected.

        </Note>

The task with the responses is saved on the **Done** page until you connect to Wi-Fi, if you set the **Submit over Wi-Fi only** option in the [settings](../settings). You need to make sure the task is submitted before the task time expires.

### How do I make changes to a task? {#makechanges}

If a task is completed but isn't sent yet, you can make changes to it:

1. Open the task on the **Done** page.

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733319521/1676733319521_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> at the top of the screen and select **Change status to reserved**.

1. Go to the **Reserved** page and make changes to the task.

1. Tap **Done**.

### How do I submit a task? {#sendtask}

Task responses are sent to the requester as soon as you have internet access. To send, you need to open the mobile app. If the **Submit over Wi-Fi only** option is chosen in the [settings](../settings), the task is sent after you connect to Wi-Fi. To submit a task over mobile internet:

1. Open the task on the **Done** page.

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733319521/1676733319521_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> at the top of the screen and choose **Submit**.

## Tips and recommendations {#tips}

The list of tasks displays all tasks, including both field and non-field tasks. To open the list of tasks, tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Available**.

To manage the list of tasks, apply filters and configure sorting:

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448913193/1676448913193_original_png.png" alt="" /> at the top of the screen.

1. Choose one or more filters. Choose the requesters you want to see in the list. Set the sorting type.

1. Tap **Apply**.

Unavailable tasks are marked with the <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733315411/1676733315411_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> icon. The condition for accessing the task is indicated next to it. Some tasks can only be performed in the browser on the [toloka.yandex.com](http://toloka.yandex.com/) website.

### How to add a task to favorites {#favourites}

Mark the tasks that you liked:

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733323602/1676733323602_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> in the task card.

2. Select **Add to favorites**.

To place your favorite tasks at the top of the list, set the **Favorites first** [sorting](#android-list/list-settings-android) type.

### How do I hide a task? {#hide}

Hide a task so that it doesn't appear in the list.

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676733323602/1676733323602_original_svg.svg" alt="" /> in the task card.

1. Select **Hide task**.

### How to change pin appearance {#changelook}

To choose which task information to display in the label:

1. Tap <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1676448834501/1676448834501_original_png.png" alt="" /> → **Settings**.

1. Open **Change pin appearance** and choose the desired option.

1. Tap **Save**.
