The history of completed tasks is available on the [Profile → History](https://toloka.yandex.com/profile/history) page.

<TolokersPriemkaContent2/>

After the review, payment for the accepted tasks is credited to your account. The amounts are indicated in the upper [Toloka](http://toloka.yandex.com/) menu  on the right <img src="https://tlk-infra-front.azureedge.net/portal-static/uploaded/images/1676733322566/1676733322566_original_svg.svg" alt="" />:

- Gray indicates the amount for tasks that are currently under review.

- Green indicates money on your Toloka account.

<TolokersPriemkaContent3/>

1. Go to the **[Profile → History](https://toloka.yandex.com/profile/history)** page.

1. Choose **Rejected** in the drop-down list above the **Completed tasks in the last 2 months** table.

1. Click the project.

1. Open the rejected task by clicking on its number.

1. Click **Contact requester** at the top of the page [Messages](http://toloka.yandex.com/messages).

1. Describe briefly why you think the task was completed correctly. The task number is attached to the message automatically.

1. Click **Submit**.
