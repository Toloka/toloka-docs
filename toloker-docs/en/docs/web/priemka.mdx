The history of completed task sets is available on the [Profile → History](https://we.toloka.ai/profile/history) page.

<TolokersPriemkaContent2/>

After the review, payment for the accepted task sets is credited to your account. The amounts are indicated in the upper [Toloka](https://toloka.ai/tolokers/en/) menu on the right <img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1677847618206/1677847618206_original_svg.svg" alt="" />:

- Gray indicates the amount for task sets that are currently under review.

- Green indicates money on your Toloka account.

<TolokersPriemkaContent3/>

1. Go to the **[Profile → History](https://we.toloka.ai/profile/history)** page.

1. Choose **Rejected** in the drop-down list above the **Completed task sets in the last 2 months** table.

1. Click the task set.

1. Open the rejected task set by clicking on its number.

1. Click **Contact requester** at the top of the page [Messages](https://we.toloka.ai/messages).

1. Describe briefly why you think the task set was completed correctly. The task set number is attached to the message automatically.

1. Click **Submit**.
