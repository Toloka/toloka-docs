<Cut title="How do I insert a calendar?">

You can see an example in the comments for this [project](https://codepen.io/Toloka_projects/pen/PVemPK). The example includes the output data format and libraries to be added.

To add libraries:

1. Click the “gear button” in project editing mode.

1. In the field that opens on the left, enter the links and press **Enter**.
