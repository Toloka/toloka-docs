<Cut title="Can I control the frequency of showing captchas to the Tolokers? Some Tolokers get a bit demotivated by that.">

The frequency of issuing [captchas](/docs/guide/captcha) is set up in the pool.

<Definition title="No">

Don't show captchas.

</Definition>

<Definition title="Low">

Show a captcha after every 20 assignments.

</Definition>

<Definition title="Average/High">

Show a captcha after every 10 assignments.

</Definition>

</Cut>
