By setting the filters, you choose Tolokers that meet your requirements to complete your task. There are filters by various criteria, and filters by skills that you can create yourself.

You can filter Tolokers by language, country, city, and so on. [Learn more](https://toloka.ai/en/docs/guide/concepts/filters)

If you give [training tasks](https://toloka.ai/en/docs/glossary#training-task) to Tolokers, you can add a skill to the pool to choose Tolokers who already completed training. [Learn more about skills](https://toloka.ai/en/docs/guide/concepts/nav)
