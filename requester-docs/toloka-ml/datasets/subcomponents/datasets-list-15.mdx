To restore the dataset,

1. Go to the [Datasets](https://tolokamodels.tech/datasets) section.

1. In the table with the dataset list, click the row of the dataset you want to restore.

1. On the dataset page, click **<img src="https://tlk-infra-front.azureedge.net/portal-static/uploaded/images/1678438762479/1678438762479_original_svg.svg" alt="Restore dataset" /> Restore** next to the dataset name.
