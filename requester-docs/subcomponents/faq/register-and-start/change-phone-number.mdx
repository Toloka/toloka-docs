<Cut title="How do I change the phone number in my account?">

To change your phone number, go to [Yandex ID](https://passport.toloka.ai/profile/phones) and change your main number.

<Note mode="note" title="Note">

If you don't have access to the old number, it takes a month to replace it. For more information about changing the phone number, see [Yandex ID Help](https://yandex.com/support/passport/authorization/phone.html#change).

</Note>

</Cut>
