<Cut title="How can I give access to my project?">

To give access to your project:

1. Open the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile) page.

1. In the **Account access** tab, click **<img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1686913467355/1686913467355_original_svg.svg" alt="Grant access" /> Grant access**.

1. Enter the case-sensitive [Yandex ID](https://id.yandex.com/about).

1. In the drop-down list, select **Full access** or **View only**.

1. Click **Grant access**.

Refer to the [Shared access to the account](/docs/guide/multiple-access) section for more information.

</Cut>
