<Cut title="How do I add money to Toloka?">

You can top up your Toloka account using a bank card or bank transfer. On the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile) page, click "Top up account", fill out the form, and you'll be redirected to the billing service. Refer to the [step-by-step instructions](/docs/guide/refill#step-by-step).

</Cut>
