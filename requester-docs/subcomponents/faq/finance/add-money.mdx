<Cut title="How do I add money to Toloka?">

You can top up your Toloka account using a bank card or bank transfer. On the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile) page, click "Connect to billing", fill out the form, and you'll see the "Top up account" button. [Learn more](/docs/guide/refill#step-by-step) with step-by-step instructions.
