## Path parameters {#path-params}

Parameter | Overview
----- | -----
**id** | **string**<br/><br/>ID of the assignment to a Toloker.
**task_suite_id** | **string**<br/><br/>ID of a task suite.
**pool_id** | **string**<br/><br/>ID of the pool that the task suite belongs to.
**user_id** | **string**<br/><br/>ID of the Toloker who was assigned the task suite.
**status** | <GetAssignmentIdTable1/>
**reward** | **integer**<br/><br/>Payment received by the Toloker.
**public_comment** | **string**<br/><br/>A comment for the Toloker.<br/><br/>Maximum length: 2048 characters.
**bonus_ids[]** | **array of strings**<br/><br/>`IDs` of bonuses issued for the task.
**tasks[]** | **array of objects**<br/><br/>[Data for the tasks](/docs/api/task-suite).

**submitted** | **string**<br/><br/>The date and time when the task suite was completed by a Toloker. The date is specified in UTC in ISO 8601 format: `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[.sss]`.
**accepted** | **string**<br/><br/>The date and time when the responses for the task suite were accepted by the requester. The date is specified in UTC in ISO 8601 format: `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[.sss]`.
**rejected** | **string**<br/><br/>The date and time when the responses for the task suite were rejected by the requester. The date is specified in UTC in ISO 8601 format: `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[.sss]`.
**skipped** | **string**<br/><br/>The date and time when the task suite was skipped by a Toloker. The date is specified in UTC in ISO 8601 format: `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[.sss]`.
**expired** | **string**<br/><br/>The date and time when the task suite expired. The date is specified in UTC in ISO 8601 format: `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[.sss]`.
