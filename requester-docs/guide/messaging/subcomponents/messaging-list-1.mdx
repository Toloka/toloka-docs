1. (Optional, required if you want to send a message to a group of Tolokers with a certain skill) [Assign a skill](/docs/guide/nav-assign) to everyone who completed your tasks. The skill should be hidden.

1. Go to the [Messages](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/messages) page and send notifications to Tolokers with the skill. To do this, click **Contact** → **Group of Tolokers** → **+ Add filter**.

1. Specify the subject of your message in the **Subject** field.

1. Fill in the **Message text** field.

1. Select **Allows replies**.

1. Click **Submit**.
