# Pool statistics

You can track task completion on the [pool](/docs/glossary#pool) page or by clicking **Statistics**. You can also get notifications by email, in the browser or on the **Messages** page. In the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile), open the **Notifications** tab and choose which notifications you want to receive and how you want to receive them.
