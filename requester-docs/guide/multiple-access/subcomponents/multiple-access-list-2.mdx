<Note mode="note" title="Note">

Only the enterprise clients with SSO authentication enabled and set up can use this. Read the [Toloka SSO authentication](/docs/guide/sso/authentication) section which contains the detailed description of this method and its restrictions.

</Note>

To give access to SSO clients, you must know their enterprise email addresses used to log in to Toloka with [SSO authentication](/docs/guide/sso/authentication).

1. Go to the [Profile → Account access](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile/access) tab.

1. Click **<img src="https://tlkfrontprod.azureedge.net/portal-production/static/uploaded/images/1686913467355/1686913467355_original_svg.svg" alt="Grant access" /> Grant access**.

1. Enter the client enterprise email address.

1. In the drop-down list, select **Full access** or **View only**.

1. Click **Grant access**.
