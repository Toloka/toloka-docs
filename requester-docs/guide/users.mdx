# Viewing Tolokers

The [Users](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/workers) page shows all Tolokers who opened at least one of your tasks. On this page, you can select Tolokers to:

- [Assign or take away a skill](/docs/guide/nav).

- [Block or unblock access](/docs/guide/ban?form-source=guide-users). For example, if you blocked a Toloker by mistake.

- [Issue a reward](/docs/guide/bonus) for good performance. Loyal Tolokers will complete your tasks more willingly.

<SelectTolokers/>

You can download the list of the chosen Tolokers. To do this, click **Download .xls** at the top right.
