To upload [file with tasks](/docs/glossary#tsv) to a [pool](/docs/guide/pool-main):

1. Go to the **Prepare and upload data** step if you're creating a pool or click the **Upload** button on the pool page.

1. Attach a prepared dataset or media files.

    <TaskUploadList3/>


    {/* <Tabs tabs={[

1. Click **Continue**.

1. Choose a way to put tasks into suites and specify the number of tasks per suite. For more information, see [Ways to group tasks in suites](/docs/guide/distribute-tasks-by-pages).

1. Click **Combine tasks into suites**.

1. Wait for the result.

To delete all the tasks in the pool, click **Delete**.

<Note mode="restriction" title="Restriction">

You can add up to one million tasks to the pool. To upload more tasks, create another pool.

</Note>
