# Topping up your account

## Deposit methods `{#method}`
You can use the following options to add funds to your Toloka account:

#### Visa or EuroCard/MasterCard.

The payment is completed within 15 minutes.

If you chose **Profile type → Legal entity** when [registering](/docs/guide/access) in Toloka, you must use a corporate bank card.

#### Bank transfer

The money transfer takes from 3 to 10 days.

When making a transfer, specify the invoice number in the payment reference.

The full list can be found on the site of the [USA Department of the Treasury](https://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/sanctions/Programs/Pages/Programs.aspx).

## Step-by-step instructions `{#step-by-step}`

1. On the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile) page, click **Top up account**.

1. If you're topping up your account for the first time, the **Connect to billing system** page will open. Follow these steps:

    1. Fill in your details.

        <Note mode="note" title="Note">

        This information will be included in accounting documents (service invoices).

        </Note>

    1. Click **Connect**.

1. Enter the transfer amount.

1. Choose the payment method.

1. Click **Top up**.

1. Enter your card details.

    <Note mode="note" title="Note">

    To avoid having to enter your card details again later, enable the **Save card for future payments** option.

    </Note>

1. Click the **Pay** button.

1. Track the receipt of money in your Toloka account on the [Profile](https://platform.toloka.ai/requester/profile) page (the **Transactions history** tab).
