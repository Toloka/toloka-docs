## Troubleshooting {#troubleshooting}

<Cut title="Stop cooperating with Toloka as a self-employed individual">

You don't have to end your cooperation with [Toloka](http://toloka.yandex.com/) if you no longer want to complete tasks. You don't have to pay the 6% tax if you stop doing tasks and withdrawing money from Toloka.

If you change your mind, you can resume completing tasks and withdrawing money at any time.

If you still want to end your cooperation with Toloka:

* Open the {% if platform == "web" %}**Profile**{% elsif platform== "android" %}**Profile**{% elsif platform == "ios" %}**{{ mobile_ios_profile.profile }}**{% endif %} tab.

* Click **Stop cooperating with Toloka as self-employed**.

* Make sure you have withdrawn all your money and are not going to perform any tasks. Click **Confirm**.

</Cut>

<Cut title="Decline the self-employment status">

<Note mode="restriction" title="Alert">

If you decline the self-employment status, you will no longer be able to complete tasks and withdraw money from your [Toloka](http://toloka.yandex.com/) account.

</Note>

You can change the tax regime:

- In the [taxpayer account](https://lknpd.nalog.ru/auth/login).
- In the My Taxes mobile app (under **Discontinue registration at any time**).

</Cut>

<Cut title="Restoring your self-employment status">

* Make sure you're registered in the [account](https://lknpd.nalog.ru/auth/login).

* [Make Toloka a partner](/docs-new/tolokers/self-employed/accept-status.dita#accept-status/section_f3t_j4p_bmb) in the account settings.

* Contact [support](/docs-new/tolokers/troubleshooting/troubleshooting.dita). In your message, specify that you want to be a self-employed Toloker and why you declined self-employment status before.

</Cut>

<Cut title="Receipts: view, cancel, or restore">

A _receipt_ is a payment document that specifies the amount of your earnings. Receipts are automatically generated each time money is withdrawn from [Toloka](http://toloka.yandex.com/).

* Go to the [**My money**](https://toloka.yandex.ru/profile/money) tab.

* Select the desired row with data in the **Actions history** table.

* Click **View receipt**.

<Note mode="restriction" title="Alert">

According to the law, once you withdraw your money, you can cancel a receipt, but **you will no longer be able to withdraw money** until the circumstances are clarified. if you have canceled a receipt by mistake, contact support.

</Note>

</Cut>
