# How to invite friends and get rewards

Invite friends to register as Tolokers and get a reward that equals 5 to 20% of their earnings in [Toloka](http://toloka.yandex.com/). Read more in the [reward rules](/docs-new/tolokers/referal-rules).

To invite a friend:

<If condition={props.platform == "web"}>

1. Open the [Referral program](https://toloka.yandex.com/profile/referral) page.
1. Copy and send the link to friends or share it on a social network.

</If><If condition={props.platform == "ios"||props.platform==="android"}>

1. Open the <If condition={props.platform == "android"}>**More → Referral program**</If><If condition={props.platform == "ios"}>**More → Referral program**</If> page.
1. Click <If condition={props.platform == "android"}>**Invite friends**</If><If condition={props.platform == "ios"}>**Invite friends**</If>.
1. Copy and send the link to friends or share it on a social network.

</If>

<Note mode="restriction" title="Alert">

You can't distribute the link in the following ways:

- Contextual advertising
- Email mailing list
- PopUp / ClickUnder
- Doorway traffic
- Incent traffic
- Toolbar
- Adult traffic

</Note>
