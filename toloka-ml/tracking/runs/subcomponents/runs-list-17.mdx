To create a run,

1. Open the model page clicking the model at the [Models → Registry](https://tolokamodels.tech/models/registry) or [Models → Collections](https://tolokamodels.tech/models/podium) page.

2. Select **Train** to train your model on your own dataset or **Infer** to apply the model to an unlabeled dataset.

3. Enter the dataset information to the fields.

4. Click **Run scenario**.
