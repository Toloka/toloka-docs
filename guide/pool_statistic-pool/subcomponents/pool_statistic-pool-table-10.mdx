The average price in USD per general task in the pool, including overlap.

Note that this is the price per task, not per suite with overlap.

<Cut title="Recommendations">

For example, [Dynamic pricing](/docs-new/guide/dynamic-pricing#section_ucl_3hl_vlb) and [Recompletion of assignments from banned users](/docs-new/guide/restore-task-overlap) increase the cost of the pool.
