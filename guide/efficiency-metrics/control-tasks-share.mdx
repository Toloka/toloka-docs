# Control tasks

This indicator shows the average ratio of control tasks to other tasks within per [task suites](/docs/glossary#task-suite). A large percentage of control tasks lets you improve the quality but, at the same time, it may make labeling more expensive.

## How to calculate

<Formula withoutContainer={true} content="$P_{control} = \frac{T_{control}}{T_{total}}\times100$"/>

where:

- <Formula withoutContainer={true} content="$P_{control}$"/> is the percentage of control tasks.

- <Formula withoutContainer={true} content="$T_{control}$"/> is the number of control tasks.

- <Formula withoutContainer={true} content="$T_{total}$"/> is the total number of tasks.

The indicator estimation criteria depends on the pool type.
